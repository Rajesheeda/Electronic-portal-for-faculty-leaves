Lbrce Portal For Faculty leaves

Today most of the staff in LBRCE are facing the problem of applying leaves and knowing whether a particular staff is absent or present on the particular date. This Staff helps staff and administration to monitor the leaves and permissions through an electronic portal. Presently paper based system is in place for leaves and permissions. This project helps LBRCE to create a web-enabled portal with several resources that help staff to move from legacy to modern system. This leaves and permissions are send in Email notification to the people involved. It has Calendar integration, Document uploading, vital information . There is a Administrative Console(s) for approval and User management and control
