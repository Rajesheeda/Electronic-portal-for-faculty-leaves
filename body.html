The International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Data Engineering (ICCIDE-2017) will be held during July 14-15, 2017, Vijayawada, India. ICCIDE-2017 is conceived as a forum for presenting and exchanging ideas and results of high quality research work in cutting edge technologies and most happening areas of computational intelligence and data engineering. It brings together the researchers from academia and industry onto a common platform and help them develop a comprehensive understanding of the challenges of technological advancements from different viewpoints. This forum also helps in fostering a healthy and vibrant relationship between academia and industry.


All the Accepted and registered papers will be published in conference proceedings by Springer.

Conference Home page http://www.iccide.in

Prospective authors are invited to submit full (original) research papers; which are NOT submitted or published or under consideration anywhere in other conferences or journals; in electronic format through Easy chair( https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=iccide2017)

Last Date of Paper Submission: 28 February 2017

If this call for paper is not of your area of interest; please forward to a researcher who is working in this area.
